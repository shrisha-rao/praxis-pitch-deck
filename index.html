Technology

Three stages. From raw data
to verified evidence.

🗂️
Stage 1 — Build
Structural Mapping
Ingest all documents. Extract entities — people, aliases, orgs, timelines — and link them into a heterogeneous knowledge graph.
Knowledge Graph · NER
📊
Stage 2 — Rank
Relevance Scoring
GNN link prediction and centrality scoring surface the top 1% of documents with the highest structural signal. No brute-force LLM pass.
GNN · Graph ML · Hybrid Retrieval
🔍
Stage 3 — Reason
Cognitive Filtering
LLM reviews only the ranked documents, producing explainable evidence with reasoning chains that lawyers can cite and courts can trust.
LLM-Assisted Review · Explainability
⚖️
Output
Verified Evidence
An evidence map, timeline, and ranked document set — grounded in structural proof, not semantic guess-work.
